Comp Sci Notes Week 7 Jobs Processes

To run a process in the background - type "&" at the end of a command line.

Suspend a foreground process by typing ctrl-z.

To bring a background job to foreground type "fg"
If there are more than one background jobs type "fg 1" or "% 1" to bring that number process to foreground. 

To stop a background process type "kill 1" to kill that numbered process. 

Type "jobs" to list all jobs.

Type "ps -f" to list all processes.
